Inocove​ ​is​ ​a​ ​platform​ for ​brands

Inocove​ ​is​ ​a​ ​platform​ ​where​ ​brands​ ​take​ ​control​ ​of​ ​the​ ​influencer​ ​marketing​ ​process.
​ ​Use​ ​platform features​ ​to​ ​reach​ ​out​ ​directly​ ​to​ ​influencers​ ​on​ ​and​ ​across​ ​multiple​ ​platforms,​
​create​ ​a​ ​campaign​ ​and hire​ ​influencers​ ​who​ ​apply​ ​for​ ​the​ ​contract,​
​or​ ​just​ ​receive​ ​submissions​ ​from​ ​influencers​ ​looking​ ​for sponsorship.

Inocove​ ​Does​ ​not​ ​take​ ​a​ ​percentage​ ​of​ ​the​ ​money​ ​passed​ ​between​
​Influencers​ ​and​ ​Brands​ ​instead​ ​we charge​ ​a​ ​standard​ ​contract​ ​fee​
​which​ ​all​ ​influencers​ ​sign​ ​agreeing​ ​to​ ​adhere​ ​to​ ​FTC​ ​regulations.

Why?

Brands who work with Influencers should use contracts to protect themselves
in the case of any FTC infractions. Inocove provides those contracts electronically.
