Why Choose Inocove?

Inocove​ ​is​ ​an​ ​easy​ ​to​ ​use​ ​platform​ ​that​ ​is​ ​100%​ ​free​ ​for​ ​the​ ​creator and​ ​offers​ ​legal​ ​protection​ ​for​ ​the​ ​brands​ ​and​ ​creators

​Protection​ ​from​ ​FTC​ ​infractions

Inocove​ ​does​ ​not​ ​charge​ ​for​ ​the​ ​use​ ​of​ ​the​ ​platform,​ ​instead​ ​the​ ​brands​ ​and​ ​agencies pay​ ​a​ ​small​ ​legal​ ​fee​ ​to​ ​cover​ ​a​ ​contract​ ​that​ ​protects​ ​them​ ​from​ ​any​ ​FTC​ ​infractions.

Streamlined Process

We​ ​offer​ ​a​ ​suite​ ​of​ ​services​ ​that​ ​any​ ​brands​ ​or​ ​agencies​ ​can​ ​use​ ​to​ ​streamline​ ​the process​ ​of​ ​finding​ ​influencers.

100% Free

Platform​ ​is​ ​100%​ ​free​ ​for​ ​influencers.

Join our community

Inocove​ ​is​ ​focussed​ ​on​ ​creating​ ​the​ ​best​ ​environment​ ​for​ ​influencers​ ​to​ ​create relationships​ ​with​ ​brands.​ ​We​ ​do​ ​not​ ​charge​ ​influencers​ ​for​ ​using​ ​our​ ​platform,​ ​and​ ​we provide​ ​an​ ​electronic​ ​contract​ ​that​ ​protects​ ​both​ ​the​ ​Influencer​ ​and​ ​the​ ​brand.
